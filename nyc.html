New York City

When you think of pizza in the US, you think of New York City. There's a reason deep dish pizza has to be called "deep dish" and not just "pizza". NYC pizza is a classic snack...quick, portable, not too filling, and absolutely delicious. Some argue it has a rival in Chicago-style deep dish, but the majority of pizza aficionados know that NYC is number one and Chicago will always be the "Second City".

Recommended NYC pizzerias: Juliana's, Joe's Pizza, Lucali

Pros

- Lighter, which allows you to have more slices

- Available by the slice, so it's perfect on the go

- Can be cooked much quicker than deep dish

- Toppings don't get lost in the sauce

Cons

- A lot of oil on the surface of the pie

- More potential for burning your mouth

- More likely to burn the pie while cooking

- Less filling than deep dish (but is this really a con?)
